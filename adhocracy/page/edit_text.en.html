1. To create a new proposal in an instance, click on one of the "new proposal" buttons.


2. First enter the title of your proposal in the header field.
Now you can describe in the big text field ("Proposal goals") why you are putting forth this proposal and what you intend to accomplish.


Now enter some tags that describe your proposal. Tags are keywords that can be attached to proposals to supply additional informations. Tags are a very helpful instrument to make proposals findable, assignable and classifiable.
By choosing significant tags you make it easy for others to find your proposal if they are interested in the same subject.
More tags can be added later on.


After clicking the save button you will be directed to the proposals overview page. You should see your new proposal in the list of proposals. This might take some time.
If your proposal doesn't show up right away, wait a little and keep refreshing the page.
